Frequently asked questions

Are Toronto public pool lane swims free?

It depends on the pool. Outdoor pool lane swims are free — just show up during a scheduled lane swim. Indoor pool lane swims usually require a paid drop-in admission (or a membership/pass). Check the pool's official page for its fees.

How many Toronto pools offer lane swimming?

LaneDuck tracks lane swim (lap swim) times at more than 370 City of Toronto public pool locations, including both indoor and outdoor pools.

What is the difference between indoor and outdoor pool lane swims?

Indoor pools run lane swims year-round, while most outdoor pools operate seasonally in the summer. Use the Indoor/Outdoor filter to see only the type you want.

What pool lengths are available?

Toronto pools come in several lengths — 25 yards (25y), 25 metres (25m), 50 yards (50y) and 50 metres (50m). LaneDuck labels each pool's length where known so you can filter for the distance you want.

How often are the swim schedules updated?

Lane swim schedules are refreshed daily from the City of Toronto's public pool data.

Can I find lane swim times near me?

Yes. Tap Sort by Distance to share your location and see the closest Toronto pools with lane swims first, plus one-tap directions.

LaneDuck is a free, independent tool and is not affiliated with the City of Toronto. Always confirm lane swim times on the official pool website before visiting.
